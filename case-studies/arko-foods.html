Arko Foods: 300,000 Followers and 4.2M Impressions in 60 Days

How Zenkai Media launched Arko Foods' digital presence from zero — growing to 300,000 followers and 4.2M monthly impressions in 60 days through organic and paid social.

The Challenge

Arko Foods was launching a new premium food brand into a crowded F&B market with zero existing digital presence. No followers, no content library, no brand voice established. They needed to build brand awareness, grow a social following, and establish authority in their category — all at launch speed, with a 60-day window before their first major retail listing.

Our Solution

We built the brand's entire social presence from scratch. Starting with a content strategy anchored on three pillars — craftsmanship and ingredients, food culture and storytelling, and community — we produced a 30-day content bank before launch. We partnered with 25 food micro-influencers for seeding, ran targeted Meta awareness campaigns to build the follower base, and created a signature content series that became the brand's most recognisable asset.

Results

By day 60: 300,000 followers across Instagram and TikTok. 4.2M monthly impressions. 12% average engagement rate (industry average: 1.8%). Eight times more organic reach than paid reach — the content was growing itself. Retail partner confirmed increased shelf allocation based on the brand's digital momentum.

The Content Strategy That Made It Work

The key decision: We spent the first two weeks building a content bank rather than posting immediately. Going live with 30 days of content ready meant the account looked established from day one — not sparse and new. First impressions on social are permanent.

The 12% engagement rate was the most significant achievement. At scale, an engagement rate above 5% is exceptional for a brand account. The reason it stayed high: we never posted content without asking "does this earn engagement?" Posting frequency was secondary to post quality. The algorithm rewarded this discipline with dramatically greater organic reach.
