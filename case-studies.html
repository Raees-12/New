Luxe Apparel was spending $30k/mo on ads with a 1.8x ROAS and declining sales. We rebuilt their entire funnel — from creative strategy to landing page CRO — and turned their ad spend into a scalable revenue engine.

READ FULL CASE STUDY
Challenge
Bleeding budget, shrinking margins

Luxe was running generic ad creatives with no clear audience segmentation. Their landing pages converted at 0.8%, and their best-sellers were buried behind underperforming collections.

Solution
Full-funnel rebuild

We restructured their Meta campaigns into 3 distinct funnel stages, produced 24 new ad creatives, rebuilt their PDPs, and added a post-purchase flow that recovered 18% of abandoned carts.

Results
$1M+ in new revenue

Month 1: ROAS climbed from 1.8x to 4.2x. Month 3: Revenue hit $600k. Month 6: $1.2M with a ROAS of 8.2x — and still scaling.
